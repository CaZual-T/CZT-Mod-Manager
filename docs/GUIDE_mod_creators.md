Forza Horizon 6: 
- YOU MUST HAVE A MEDIA OR MEDIAPC FOLDER IN YOUR UPLOADED FILE.
  - Example: CustomRX7Mod.rar inside should be -> mediapc/cars/MAZ_RX7_92.zip (mediapc can also be media)
    - the easiest way to do this is by creating a mediapc folder on your desktop,
    - inside the mediapc folder add the required subfolder like camera, cars, controls etc. (if needed)
    - drag your created mod inside the subfolder.
    - zip the mediafolder on your desktop and rename it to a name of your choice. UPLOAD THAT.

- long story short, put yout mods in the correct scope before zipping and uploading them. most creators already do this.
